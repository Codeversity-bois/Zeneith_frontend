About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our Core Experience team. In this role, you will be responsible for defining the user experience for millions of users worldwide. You will work closely with product management, engineering, and research to design simple, elegant, and powerful solutions to complex problems.

Responsibilities

  • Lead design projects across the entire product lifecycle and multiple product launches.
  • Create high-quality design deliverables, from wireframes and user flows to high-fidelity mockups and prototypes.
  • Partner with engineering to ensure high-quality implementation and user experience.
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to the design culture at Google.

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in product design, UI/UX, or related field.
  • Strong portfolio showcasing your design process and problem-solving skills.
  • Proficiency in Figma, prototyping tools, and design systems.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to articulate design decisions.
